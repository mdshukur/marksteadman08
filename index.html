Bio/About Us

Mindful Chess was founded in 2015 with the goal to improve access to chess teaching for students across London. We started off teaching a single group of 12 students once a week. Through schools and parents sharing information about our unique approach, our program has grown to now teach over 500 students a week in schools all across the UK. As well as our group lessons, we've recognised how productive 1:1 sessions are for adults and children. We now teach individuals ranging from the age of 4 to 104 across 3 different continents. teach children ages from 5+ and adults keen to learn or looking to get a new perspective on their game.
